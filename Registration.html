Admissions


COME VISIT US

Prospective families are encouraged to set up a private visit or tour during an open house. To request information and plan a visit, contact us at (401) 433-3196, ccns@eccriverside.org or ccnsinfo@eccriverside.org.

SUBMIT A REGISTRATION PACKET WITH A NON-REFUNDABLE $100 REGISTRATION FEE

View and print a registration form here and submit it with your registration fee.

COMPLETE AND SUBMIT ALL ENROLLMENT FORMS

Once registered, you will receive enrollment forms via mail or email. These forms should be completed and returned to the school prior to the first day of classes.

ATTEND PARENT ORIENTATION AND MEET THE STAFF

The parent orientation is scheduled in late summer prior to the first day of school. At the orientation (parents only), teachers present a brief overview of the CCNS program and policies. A Q&A session allows opportunity for parents to ask any unanswered questions.
