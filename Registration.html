Registration for the following school year begins in January. Enrollment for current families is held during the first two weeks of that month. Open enrollment begins the third week. As space is limited, early registration is advised.

Curious about what to do next?

COME VISIT US

Families are encouraged to set up a tour or visit during an open house. To request information and plan a visit, contact us at (401) 433-3196 or ccns@eccriverside.org.

SUBMIT AN APPLICATION WITH A NON-REFUNDABLE $100 REGISTRATION FEE

Download an application form here and submit it with your registration fee.

COMPLETE AND SUBMIT ALL ENROLLMENT FORMS

Once registered, you will receive enrollment forms via mail or email. These forms should be filled out and returned to the school prior to the first day of classes.
