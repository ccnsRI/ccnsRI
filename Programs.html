Threes

Children who turn three by September 1st are eligible to enter the two-day program meeting on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 8:30 to 12:00. An extended day option running from 12:00 to 2:30 is available at an additional cost.

Fours

Children who turn four by September 1st are eligible to enter the three-day program meeting on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays from 8:30 to 12:00. An extended day option running from 12:00 to 2:30 is available at an additional cost.

Extended Day Option

An extended day program is offered. This program runs from 12:00 to 2:30 on the days your child's program meets. Families can opt to enroll their child on one or more days of their given program.
