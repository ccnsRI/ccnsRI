OUR MISSION

To provide a safe, nurturing and engaging environment that meets the individual needs of children and families in our community. Through purposeful play and a developmentally centered curriculum, our students have opportunities to grow socially, emotionally, spiritually and academically in a Christian environment.



Established in 1972, CCNS has served the greater East Providence community by faithfully providing a play-based, developmentally centered preschool education in a vibrant Christian environment.

Students are given opportunities to engage with peers and staff in purposeful play that is designed to encourage growth and development socially, emotionally, spiritually and academically. We strive to guide and nurture children, in partnership with their families by caring for the whole child.
